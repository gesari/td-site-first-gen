Companions

Companion NPCs are entities that accompany dreamers and act in concert with them. This includes an Animist's Avatar, an Operative's Turrets, and any constructs and spirits summoned via learned collegiate architectures.

Avatar

Like the Spiritist themself, their Avatar is highly customizable. Each Avatar has its own unique Style, chosen from the list of available Dreamers; however, since an Avatar does not wield a focus of their own, if they choose Farseer Style, the Projected Focus passive instead allows the Avatar to function as the point of origin of their Spiritist's actions and vice versa. Their stats are also selected similarly to the Spiritist's, with a variance in the amount of points and the stats available: they receive 3 points to allocate at level 1, and an additional 1 point every level thereafter. These points may be invested into any of the Primary stats as with normal dreamers, but since Avatars do not have the ability to create spell architecture like a dreamer, they do not have access to Secondary stats or Collegiate Training related to azaian magic.

Companion Enhancements

Certain Skills will modify existing Companions, empowering them greatly. These actions do not create a new companion NPC, but rather alter the stats of an existing one.

Imbued Constructs

  • Gain access to Secondary stats and Collegiates relating to azaian magic.
  • Gain access to Free-Cast action.
  • Gain access to Status Effect: Rent.
  • Increase Ability Range to 25.
  • Increase Action Economy to 3.

Invested Spirits

  • Gain access to Status Effect: Projected.
  • Increase Action Economy to 3.
  • Changes to other stats based on the form the Spirit was placed in, as determined by the GM and Players.

Simulacra

  • Vitality and Shield are unaffected.
  • Other stats increase to mirror the Operative's if they are lower.
  • Available Skills mirror that of the Operative.
