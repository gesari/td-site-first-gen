Exploration

There are many miscellaneous details of traveling throughout the game space that don't necessarily fall into other categories, or are small enough as to not warrant one of their own. That data will be stored here.

Impact Damage

When an entity's movement is abruptly halted (e.g. hitting the ground after a long fall, being thrown into an obstacle by a spell effect), they suffer 1 damage per unit of distance traveled prior to impact.

Invisibility

When rendered invisible, a dreamer has a +5 Polarity bonus to both dice pools for every check in which their invisibility would be an advantage. All attempts to perceive the entity directly receive a -5 Polarity penalty.

If an invisible entity uses a Skill, Free-Casts, or channels azai in any way, they must momentarily pull back the veil of the invisibility effect, revealing themself for the duration of the action before vanishing again. The same invisibility effect will resume once they finish acting unless something specific about it would require its termination as part of the action taking place.

Concealment

When an entity is either partially or fully concealed, they receive a bonus to Defense for actions and abilities targeting them. Partially concealed entities (e.g. someone peeking around a door or standing up behind a defensive barricade) receive a +5 bonus to Defense; fully concealed entities (e.g. someone who is invisible, actively camouflaged in a hiding spot, sheltering behind a barricade or shield) receive a +10 bonus.

Units

A "unit" is the base unit of length within the game space. Players can assume that 1 unit is roughly equivalent to 1 meter (or 1 yard, if that is a more familiar unit of measurement).

Physical Range

An entity is considered within physical range of another if they are less than 1 unit apart.

Rest / Recuperation

Characters can recover from overexertion and harrowing experiences intermittently throughout the day, or with a more prolonged period of rest.

Short Rests

Anytime the characters are not actively engaged in an activity and have at least 15 minutes to catch their breath, they may take a Short Rest. During a Short Rest, their Shield recharges to its maximum value and all abilities finish their Recharge.

Long Rests

Assuming a dreamer can find a 6+ hour period to rest, they may take a Long Rest. Long Rests confer all the benefits of a Short Rest, and fully restore the dreamer's Vitality as well.

Factions

Every organization the Players might interact with in the game space will have its own agendas which may or may not align with the PCs. All members of the Legion are accorded the courtesy of Friendly relations as a baseline, but greater esteem, and thus greater access to the more exclusive parts of their own archives, are reserved for those who earn it.

Standing

There are four levels of clearance, or standing, a dreamer may achieve relative to each Court, Guild, Order, or other organization, they encounter.

Friend

Anyone the Court/Guild is willing to trade or otherwise interact with will generally fall under this tier.

Member

Unsurprisingly, members of a faction are held in higher esteem than those with whom they are unfamiliar.

Trusted

This tier is for those given special consideration by a faction. Those who hold high standing within its ranks, or those who have earned great trust and/or friendship.
