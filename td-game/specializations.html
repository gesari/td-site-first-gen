Specializations

There are 9 specializations in the game, each associated with a particular college of magic. A specialization is not meant to be a total encapsulation of the college they are based upon, but rather a single interpretation of its potential.

Specialization Tree

Every specialization's progression tree will look identical: a 10-node triangle, which can be traversed in a manner of the Player's choosing. Choosing one's specialization will immediately unlock the tree's top node (1-1), conferring immediate benefits. Future nodes will be unlocked as part of the leveling process, with one available node of the Player's choice being unlocked every time their character levels up. Since there are 10 levels corresponding to 10 nodes, a maximum-level character will have unlocked the entire tree.

Each node on the Specialization Tree has a numerical notation for determining its place in the tree, listed in a Tier-Number format: the first number denotes the ability’s tier, and the second denotes its number within the tier. Thus, the top node of each tree would be notated as 1-1, because it is in the first tier and it is the first (and, in this case, only) node within that tier. Down and left from that is node 2-1: the first node in the second tier. To its right is node 2-2, and so on. It is intended that every Spec’s 3-2 ability is meant to greatly enhance that specialization’s capabilities, generally by enhancing the benefits of its 1-1 node.

Every progression node will have some sort of prerequisite, but requirements are consistent across specializations: in order to unlock a node in the tree, all connecting nodes above it will need to be unlocked. Thus, both second-tier nodes are immediately available at level 2, since the only higher node either is connected to is the initial node unlocked during Character Creation. The middle node of the third tier, however, is connected to both second-tier nodes, meaning a Player would need to unlock both before they could unlock it.

Alchemist

College of Transmutation Specialist

Alchemists are uniquely capable at seeing the Dream for seeing the dream as it fundamentally is: all phenomena arise directly from the azonai via the interplay of azai and azon. Deep understanding of these mechanics allow Alchemists to reshape themselves and the Dream around them on a fundamental level.

Internal Alchemy | 1-1
Passive Benefit | Alchemist Ability
Prerequisite Alchemist
Description Similar to dreamers bearing a Visionary attunement, the Alchemist has come to understand transmutation magics on a deeply personal level. Though not all Alchemists are Visionaries, they, too, can change any of their physical characteristics. Uniquely, Alchemists experiment with altering their astral body along with their physical one, allowing them to fully embody each form they take.
The Alchemist has a bonus to Transmutation magic equal to their level, in addition to the benefits of their d6 pool. When they change forms, they may choose to apply any amount of this bonus to another Secondary Stat or Collegiate Training instead; this does not confer the Polarity of their d6 pool, only the flat bonus.
Vital Technique | 2-1
Channeled Action | Alchemist Ability
Modifiers + Recovery, Transmutation
Prerequisite Internal Alchemy
Description The Alchemist executes a Recharge action for free immediately and again at the beginning of their Faction's turn while this effect is being Channeled. If their Shields are full, the result of the action can be applied to the Alchemist's Vitality.
Stalwart Technique | 2-2
Channeled Action | Alchemist Ability
Modifiers + Unification, Transmutation
Prerequisite Internal Alchemy
Description While channeling, the Alchemist fortifies their body, allowing them to absorb an amount of damage each Round depending on the ability's Degree of Success.
Partial Success Absorb an amount of incoming damage equal to half Potency per Round.
Full Success Absorb an amount of incoming damage equal to full Potency per Round.
Plus +2 Potency Bonus
Mend | 3-1
Standard Action | Alchemist Ability
Modifiers + Unification, Transmutation
Prerequisite Vital Technique
Description The Alchemist instantiates a reactionary ward to prevent harm from coming to a target. The ward reacts to any incoming damage, restoring Shield and/or Vitality at half Potency per trigger. Only one instance of this ability's effect can be present on a target at a time. This ability can also be used on complex inorganic objects to prevent their destruction / preserve their functionality.
Partial Success The ward’s heal triggers once.
Full Success The ward’s heal triggers a number of times equal to half Potency.
Plus The ward’s heal triggers a number of times equal to full Potency.
Ascended Form | 3-2
Channeled Recharge Action (12) | Alchemist Ability
Prerequisite Spiritist
Description The Alchemist transforms into a unique form. While in this form, the Alchemist's Transmutation bonus from Internal Alchemy is doubled, and may be distributed among as many applicable stats as they choose. All checks made by the Alchemist while this form is maintained may apply the d6 Polarity of their Transmutation Collegiate if it is higher than the one being checked. Recharge 12, post termination.
While Channeling their Ascended Form, the Alchemist can maintain either their Vital or Stalwart Techniques for free, and may swap between which is active with an Action. If the Alchemist is Channeling either Technique when they cast Ascended Form, that effect is maintained.

Adept Shifter - Passive Bonus

The Alchemist has become especially adept at altering their own form and transmuting ambient azai into living tissue. When shapeshifting, they may now displace or accrete mass, altering their size anywhere in between a tenth and triple their normal form.

Arcanist

College of Conduction Specialist

Arcanists train their astral bodies rigorously, focused on increasing the range of their spells and tapping directly into currents of the azaian Flow: thus empowered, they are able to conduct amounts of azai beyond what other dreamers could ever conceive. With enough training, they are able to use their own bodies as a point of confluence for multiple currents, effectively doubling their Potency and Secondary stats while they are able to maintain the effect.

Astral Empowerment | 1-1
Passive Benefit | Arcanist Ability
Prerequisite Arcanist
Description The Arcanist's connection to, and control over, their Astral body has been enhanced through training. Their ability range is increased by an amount equal to their level.

Flow Current Connections

In order to exceed their limitations, Arcanists learn to tap into Flow Currents as a way of bolstering their magic. While empowered by a Current, all of the Arcanist's ability and spellcasting checks gain a static bonus equal to their level. A Current connection lasts until expended, at which point the Arcanist must endeavor to re-establish the connection.

Every time the Arcanist casts an ability or magical effect without an active Current connection, they can roll a d20 for free with no modifiers to passively attempt to re-establish a connection. The Arcanist must roll a natural 20 to succeed on their first attempt, with each successive attempt having its difficulty reduced by 1 until a successful connection is established. Once a current has been replenished, the difficulty resets to 20. A Current connection can be re-established without checks over the course of either a Short or Long Rest.

Loft | 2-1
Unique Action | Arcanist Ability
Modifiers + Projection, Conduction
Prerequisite Astral Empowerment
Description The Arcanist manipulate the local currents to manifest a platform of solid azon large enough to bear a single entity. The Loft platform can fly, has a Movement speed equal to the Arcanist, and lasts until it is either destroyed or dismissed by the Arcanist. When the Arcanist executes a Movement action, they may choose to apply the Movement to either themself or the Loft platform. Entities riding the Loft platform may also use their Movement actions in this way, so long as the Arcanist would allow them to do so.
Partial Success The Loft Pad instantiates and may be ridden.
Full Success Entities who dismount the Loft Pad in flight, or are riding when it is destroyed, are targeted with a charge of the Buoy spell at a Full degree as though the Arcanist had cast it with an action, which lasts until they land.
Plus The Loft Pad executes one free action per Round at the beginning of the Arcanist's Faction Turn.
Arc Lance | 2-2
Unique Action | Arcanist Ability
Modifiers + Attack / Projection, Conduction | - Defense, Conduction
Prerequisite Astral Empowerment
Description The Arcanist must have an active Current connection in order to use this ability.
The Arcanist draws in as much power as possible from their Current connection and channels it into a burst of energy at a point within range. The Arc Lance deals damage to all targets within 3 units of its impact.
Drawing so much azai in such a short amount of time dries up and severs the Arcanist's active Current connection. If Arc Lance is cast in the midst of a Confluence, the Confluence is also ended as though it had been broken by damage.
Partial Success Damage target entity at full Potency.
Full Success Damage target entity at double Potency.
Plus Afflict target entity with stacks of Status Effect: Sundered equal to base Potency.
Azonian Anchor | 3-1
Standard Action | Arcanist Ability
Modifiers + Projection, Conduction | - Defense, Conduction
Prerequisite Loft
Description The Arcanist binds a target in magical chains, hindering their movement.
Partial Success Afflict target entity with stacks of Status Effect: Entangled equal to half Potency.
Full Success Afflict target entity with stacks of Status Effect: Entangled equal to full Potency.
Plus This ability receives a +2 bonus to Potency.
Confluence | 3-2
Unique Action | Arcanist Ability
Modifiers + Attack / Projection, Conduction | - Defense, Conduction
Prerequisite Loft, Arc Lance
Description The Arcanist pulls upon the local currents of azai, entangling them with their active Current connection and amplifying it. The Confluence manifests as a Shield separate from, but with a value equal to the Arcanist's Shield maximum, and which surrounds the Arcanist and distorts space within 2 units of them. Entities within the spatial distortion suffer a static modifier equal to the Arcanist's level which is applied to all checks they make as a penalty and all checks made against them as a bonus. While the Arcanist remains within the Confluence, their Potency and Astral Empowerment bonuses are doubled.
The Confluence lasts until the Arcanist chooses to terminate it, they leave its Shielded area, or its Shield is depleted. Though the Confluence is stationary by default, it is capable of moving through space if instantiated upon a moving object, such as the Arcanist's Loft Pad. Once ended, the Arcanist loses hold of their active Current connection.
If the Confluence barrier is broken by damage, it explodes outwards, damaging entities within its distortion field. The check for the explosion against these entities is made as though the Confluence were still active.
Partial Success Damage target entity at half Potency.
Full Success Damage target entity at full Potency.
Plus Move target entity a number of units equal to damage they were dealt.
Replication Matrix | 3-3
Recharge Action (11-12) | Arcanist Ability
Modifiers + Projection, Conduction
Prerequisite Arc Lance
Description The Arcanist primes a matrix of azaian architecture for a future spell. Any single target spell or ability cast through it is replicated according to the ability's Degree of Success. Spells replicated in this way can target separate entities, elected by the caster at the time of replication. The Arcanist may conjure this Matrix anywhere within their ability range, where it remains stationary and endures until it is used; this location serves as the point of origin for any spells it outputs, and has a range equal to the Arcanist. The Replication Matrix is capable of copying spells fed into it by either the Arcanist or any of their allies. Recharge 11-12.
Partial Success The matrix duplicates the spell passed through it.
Full Success The matrix replicates the spell passed through it a number of times equal to half Potency.
Plus The matrix replicates the spell passed through it a number of times equal to full Potency.
Singularity | 4-1
Channeled Action | Arcanist Ability
Modifiers + Attack / Projection, Conduction | - Defense, Conduction
Prerequisite Azonian Anchor
Description The Arcanist manifests a stable Singularity which pulls entities within a 5 unit radius towards its center. Entities which reach its center are immediately afflicted with Status Effect: Immobilized; while Immobilized, any unit of movement which would have been caused by this ability is converted to damage at a 1:2 ratio.
When the Arcanist manifests the Singularity a check is made against all entities inside immediately to determine its effects upon them; if any entities are adjacent to its center, the check against them must result in at least a Partial Degree for the entity to become Immobilized. While the Arcanist maintains their Channel, this check may be repeated against any entity the first time they enter its space during a Round, and again against all entities within at the start of a new Round.
Partial Success Move target entity 1 unit towards the center of the Singularity.
Full Success Move target entity a number of units equal to half Potency towards the center of the Singularity.
Plus Move target entity a number of units equal to full Potency towards the center of the Singularity.
Counterspell | 4-2
Reactive Recharge Action (5-12) | Arcanist Ability
Modifiers + Attack / Projection, Conduction | - Defense, Conduction
Prerequisite Azonian Anchor, Confluence
Description The Arcanist attempts to sabotage an entity's action. This ability can only be used as a Reaction to another entity's action: it applies a negative modifier to the check in order to influence that action's Degree of Success. Recharge 5-12.
Partial Success Apply a static penalty to the check equal to full Potency.
Full Success Apply a static penalty to the check equal to double Potency.
Plus Targeted check cannot achieve higher than a Fail Degree.
Azaian Density | 4-3
Unique Action | Arcanist Ability
Prerequisite Confluence, Replication Matrix
Description Once per Long Rest, when the Arcanist does not have an active Current connection, they may use this ability to immediately re-establish a Current connection. The next check made after using this ability is treated as though the result of the Arcanist's d20 pool was a 20.

Azaian Prowess - Passive Bonus

When the Arcanist loses their active Current connection, the difficulty threshold for re-establishing it begins at 18 instead of 20.

Heaven's Reach | 4-4
Recharge Action (9-12) | Arcanist Ability
Modifiers + Attack / Projection, Conduction | - Defense, Conduction
Prerequisite Replication Matrix

Conduit

College of Harmonics Specialist

Conduits study the generative cycles of the Dream. In a manner similar to the Ascendants who uphold the Pact, the Conduit has attuned themselves to currents of azai between and within the dimensions of the Dream, ensuring its balance. Acting as an intermediary for these forces manifests an Aura which permeates the space around them.

Aura | 1-1
Prerequisite Conduit
Description The Conduit's Aura has a base radius of 2 units; friendly entities within receive a +1 Polarity bonus to their d6 pool, and have a minimum d6 Polarity of 1 for checks involving Collegiates in which they are at least Practiced. Both the Aura's radius and Polarity bonuses increase at specific level thresholds: 3 unit radius, +2 d6 Polarity at level 4; 4 unit radius, +3 d6 Polarity at level 7; 6 unit radius, +4 d6 Polarity at level 10. Bonuses which would exceed the Polarity cap are instead converted into static bonuses on checks.
Synergism | 2-1
Unique Action | Conduit Ability
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics
Prerequisite Aura
Description The Conduit has learned how to regulate and fortify the internal flow of azai in entities. This blessing can only be placed upon entities within their Aura, but once active it will endure for as long as the Conduit desires so long as they remain within the same realm. The Conduit may only confer a single Synergism blessing at a time, and may overwrite existing instances of the effect by using another action to apply it to another entity.
Partial Success Confer a bonus to one secondary stat equal to half Potency.
Full Success Confer a bonus to two secondary stats equal to half Potency.
Plus The bonus increases to full Potency while within the Conduit's Aura.
Replenishing State | 2-2
Unique Action | Aura State | Conduit Ability
Prerequisite Aura
Description Allies who begin a Round inside the Conduit's Aura may execute a free Recharge action. Other allies may gain this benefit the first time they enter the Conduit's Aura each Round. Recharge actions triggered in this way may use the Conduit's statistics if the entity chooses.
An Aura State confers benefits to friendly entities within the Conduit's Aura in addition to its base Polarity bonus. Only one Aura State may be active at a time.
Intercede| 3-1
Reactive Action | Conduit Ability
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics
Prerequisite Synergism
Description The Conduit condenses the ambient azai of their Aura around an entity within, enhancing their Defense rating until the end of the Round. This ability may be used in response to another action in order to alter its Degree of Success.
Partial Success Confer a defense bonus equal to half Potency.
Full Success Confer a defense bonus equal to full Potency.
Plus This ability receives a +2 bonus to Potency; if the target of this ability is the Conduit’s Synergism target, the bonus increases to +6.
Convection | 3-2
Unique Action | Conduit Ability
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics
Prerequisite Synergism, Replenishing State
Description The Conduit channels the azai within their Aura through an entity within to enhance an action they are in the process of attempting by adding a static modifier to the check. If this ability is being used to empower the Conduit themself, they may use it for free as part of the action they are enhancing. The Conduit expends their Aura Reservoir to power this ability, and the bonus conferred is directly proportional to the amount of their Reservoir expended.
The Conduit's Aura Reservoir must be above 0 in order to use Convection. They may expend any amount of their current Reservoir to power the ability.
Partial Success Reservoir converts to static bonus at 1:1 ratio.
Full Success Reservoir converts to static bonus at 1:1 ratio.
Plus Reservoir converts to static bonus at 1:2 ratio.

Aura Reservoir - Passive Bonus

Whenever anyone within the Conduit's Aura suffers damage, their Aura Reservoir increases by an amount equal to the damage dealt to Shields by the attack, doubled if the Shield breaks. The Conduit's Aura Reservoir has a maximum capacity equal to double their level.

Unique Action | Aura State | Conduit Ability
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics | - Adaptation / Defense, Harmonics
Prerequisite Replenishing State
Description Movement actions initiated from within the Conduit's aura by friendly entities have their distance doubled. Whenever a hostile entity moves into or within the Conduit's Aura, it triggers a free check from the Conduit against them.
An Aura State confers benefits to friendly entities within the Conduit's Aura in addition to its base Polarity bonus. Only one Aura State may be active at a time.
Partial Success Reduce the remaining distance of target entity's current Movement action by 1 unit.
Full Success Reduce target entity’s available Movement to 0.
Plus Afflict target entity with a stack of Status Effect: Entangled.
Paean | 4-1
Channeled Action | Conduit Ability
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics
Prerequisite Intercede

The amount restored is determined via a check that is rolled immediately, and again at the start of every new Round during which it is Channeled, and every application of the ability for that Round refers to the initial check. When the Conduit makes this check, they may execute a Counter action for free on any entity within their Aura, or their Synergism target regardless of location.
Partial Success Heal target entity at half Potency.
Full Success Heal target entity at full Potency.
Plus This ability receives a +4 bonus to Potency.
Cleanse | 4-2
Recharge Action (10-12) | Conduit Ability
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics
Prerequisite Intercede, Convection
Description The Conduit discharges energy accreted by their Aura, resetting their Aura State to neutral. All allied entities within their Aura are healed. The Conduit may expend points from their Aura Reservoir to amplify this effect. Recharge 10-12.
Partial Success Heal target entity at half Potency; 2:1 conversion of Reservoir points to healing.
Full Success Heal target entity at full Potency; 1:1 conversion of Reservoir points to healing.
Plus This ability receives a +2 bonus to Potency; 1:2 conversion of Reservoir points to healing.
Siphoning State | 4-3
Unique Action | Aura State | Conduit Ability
Prerequisite Convection, Kinetic State
Description While this Aura State is active, actions made by entities benefiting from the Conduit's Aura which result in a Plus Degree also receive a +2 Potency bonus. Actions thus empowered may bypass an entity's Shields if they have a damage component and the executor so chooses. When an action receives this Aura State's enhancement, the Conduit's Reservoir increases by 2.
An Aura State confers benefits to friendly entities within the Conduit's Aura in addition to its base Polarity bonus. Only one Aura State may be active at a time.
Reflexive State | 4-4
Unique Action | Aura State | Conduit Ability
Modifiers + Adaptation / Attack, Harmonics | - Defense, Harmonics
Prerequisite Kinetic State
Description While this Aura State is active, entities benefiting from the Conduit's Aura may use an additional Reaction each Round. Hostile entities within the Aura attempting to use a Reaction trigger a free check against them by the Conduit.
An Aura State confers benefits to friendly entities within the Conduit's Aura in addition to its base Polarity bonus. Only one Aura State may be active at a time.
Partial Success The check receives a -1 penalty to d6 Polarity.
Full Success The check receives a -1 penalty to Polarity for both dice pools.
Plus The check cannot achieve a higher Degree than Fail.
