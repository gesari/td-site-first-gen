Spell Architecture Catalog

This section is meant to be an exhaustive list of every spell within the game, organized according to their School, College, and Spell Tier classifications.

Adaptation School Architectures

The College of Animism

First Spell Tier

Confusion
Standard Action | Animism Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation / Attack, Animism | - Defense, Animism
Prerequisite none
Description Rapidly expand a target's perception, overloading their mind with information.
Partial Success Decrease target entity’s Action Economy by 1 next Round.
Full Success Restrict target entity's ability to take Reactions until the end of next Round.
Plus Afflict target entity with Status Effect: Confused.
Psionic Link
Channeled Action | Animism Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Animism
Prerequisite none
Description Connect with another entity's spirit to share thoughts and knowledge. This can enable the caster to effectively communicate with any sentient entity.
Partial Success The caster establishes a one-way connection between them and the target; the caster chooses which entity may broadcast.
Full Success The caster establishes a two-way connection between them and the target: both may communicate freely with the other.
Plus The caster gains complete control of the Link, and may swap it between willing target entities as an Action so long as the Channel is maintained.

Second Spell Tier

Call Spirit
Standard Action | Animism Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Animism
Prerequisite Symbiotic Bond
Description Beseech a wandering spirit to aid you. The spirit is inherently friendly to the caster unless they or their allies take a hostile action against it. It has its own Action Economy and acts as part of the caster's Faction Turn.
Partial Success The spirit answers the call, but manifests slowly. It may not act until the following Round.
Full Success The spirit arrives and may act immediately.
Plus The spirit gains a +1 Polarity bonus to both dice pools for all checks.
Symbiotic Bond
Channeled Action | Animism Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Animism
Prerequisite Psionic Link
Description Connect two spirits, allowing separate entities to share thoughts and knowledge to pool their talents. The targeted entity may apply the caster's modifier for a chosen Collegiate Training as though it was their own, and vice versa. These shared bonuses are elected by the caster at the time of the bond's instantiation. (e.g. the caster has a high Animism modifier, and the target has a high Perception modifier; when the spell is cast, the two highest modifiers for each skill apply to both the caster and targeted entity).
Partial Success The caster instantiates a one-way bond between them and the target; the caster elects which entity receives which bonus.
Full Success The caster instantiates a two-way bond between them and the target; the caster and target entity both receive bonuses.
Plus The caster amplifies the bond: both caster and target entity receive a +1 Polarity bonus to their d6 pool for affected checks.
Thoughtsteal
Standard Action | Animism Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation / Attack, Animism | - Defense, Animism
Prerequisite Confusion
Description Retrieve a memory from an entity.
Partial Success The caster retrieves most of the target memory, but the entity is unpleasantly aware of the theft.
Full Success The caster retrieves the memory without detection or disturbance to the target.
Plus The caster may elect to erase or alter the target entity's memory of the event.

Third Spell Tier

Thoughtblur
Standard Action | Animism Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation / Attack, Animism | - Defense, Animism
Prerequisite Thoughtsteal
Description Influence the emotional state of entities in an area.
Partial Success The spell neutralizes the entity's emotional state; hostile entities are not completely pacified, but will seek to incapacitate and capture their targets rather than maim and/or kill them.
Full Success The spell empties the entity's short term memory, causing them to forget the present circumstance.
Plus The caster may alter or fully supplant the affected memories.
Communion
Standard Action | Animism Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Animism
Prerequisite Call Spirit
Description Relive a memory of something that happened within the space the caster occupies, as it was seen by the spirits of the place. Though similar to Augury's Oracle spell, the effect is subtly different: Oracle shows the caster a more neutral vision, simply a recorded memory of what happened, similar to watching security footage. Communion gives a more direct insight into how an entity felt about an event, similar to reading someone's diary entry about a particular event.
Partial Success The caster gains limited access to the memory they are looking for: they can see important details devoid of additional context (e.g. the end of an altercation but not the inciting incident).
Full Success The caster witnesses a vision of the entire event as an outside observer.
Plus The caster’s experience of the event is fully immersive and interactive.
Invest Spirit
Standard Action | Animism Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Animism
Prerequisite Call Spirit
Description Permanently bind a willing spirit to an inanimate object (e.g. genie in a bottle, or a spirit animating a skeleton/corpse/golem).
Partial Success The investiture happens but is not permanent: the spirit must maintain an active Channel in order to remain bound.
Full Success The investiture occurs and endures as intended.
Plus The spirit gains a bonus of +1 Polarity for all rolls to both dice pools.

The College of Harmonics

First Spell Tier

Cure
Standard Action | Harmonics Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics
Prerequisite Renew
Description Remove negative status effects from a target.
Partial Success Remove one status effect from target entity.
Full Success Remove two status effects from target entity.
Plus The next status effect that would be applied to the target is prevented.
Feign Death
Channeled Action | Harmonics Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics
Prerequisite none
Description The caster attempts to place themself in a state of deep rest, to the point of appearing dead. Hostile actions against them trigger Channel checks with a -5 d20 Polarity penalty.
Partial Success The spell succeeds, but the caster loses themselves within the spell and will be unable to purposely break the channel themselves until they have maintained it long enough to complete a Long Rest.
Full Success The spell succeeds and the caster retains control of their Channel.
Plus The caster may awaken with the benefits of a Long Rest if they complete a Short Rest while maintaining the Channel.
Renew
Standard Action | Harmonics Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics
Prerequisite none
Description Return an entity/object to its original form. Heal entities, with a chance to cure them of any additional lingering ailments. Vitality is restored first, with any healing beyond max Vitality converting into Shield.
Partial Success Restore Vitality of target entity equal to half Potency.
Full Success Restore Vitality of target entity equal to full Potency.
Plus Target entity is either purged of a single status effect, or, if they are not suffering from an active status effect, they instead receive a +1 d20 Polarity bonus on their next check.

Second Spell Tier

Regenerate
Standard Action | Harmonics Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics
Prerequisite Resurgence
Description Bolster an entity's natural healing processes, placing a spell on them that will heal them when they execute their next action(s).
Partial Success The enchantment has 1 trigger, healing for half Potency.
Full Success The enchantment has 2 triggers, healing for half Potency.
Plus The enchantment has 3 triggers, healing for full Potency.
Restore
Standard Action | Harmonics Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics
Prerequisite Siphon
Description Return a dead or unconscious entity to a previous version of itself. Revive dead targets, or reduce the effects of aging on living ones.
Partial Success Target entity is restored to life at 1 Vitality.
Full Success Half of target entity's Vitality is restored.
Plus Target is returned to life with full Vitality and Shield.
Resurgence
Standard Action | Harmonics Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics
Prerequisite Renew
Description Heal entities and/or repair objects within range.
Partial Success Heal 1 target at half Potency.
Full Success Heal 3 targets at half Potency.
Plus Heal 3 targets at full Potency.
Siphon
Standard Action | Harmonics Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation / Attack, Harmonics | - Defense, Harmonics
Prerequisite Regenerate
Description Deal damage and siphon energy from a target, which can later be dispersed as healing to another entity via another action. If the caster is a Conduit, the damage dealt is added to their Aura Reservoir; other casters may use their Reservoir only for healing. Non-Conduit dreamers may dispense their Reservoir via an action without a check; so long as they are in range of their intended target, they may spend an action and apply the healing. A character's Reservoir has a capacity equal to their level, but this may be temporarily exceeded in the event of a Plus Degree.
Partial Success Target entity is damaged at half Potency; damage converted into Reservoir at a 1:1 ratio.
Full Success Target entity is damaged at full Potency; damage converted into Reservoir at a 1:1 ratio.
Plus Target entity is damaged at full Potency; damage converted into Reservoir at a 1:2 ratio.

Third Spell Tier

Group Cure
Standard Action | Harmonics Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics
Prerequisite Vital Surge
Description Remove negative status effects from allies in range.
Partial Success Target entities are cured of a single status effect.
Full Success Target entities are cured of two status effects.
Plus Target entities receive a heal with a Potency value equal to the total number of status effects purged.
Harvest
Channeled Action | Harmonics Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation / Attack, Harmonics | - Defense, Harmonics
Prerequisite Siphon
Description Create an area effect with a 4 unit radius that drains the life from targets within it. Damage dealt to targets causes their life force to leech from their bodies into the air around them, creating a haze which Obscures entities within. Damage dealt is converted and stored within the caster's Reservoir. If the caster chooses to terminate the channel of this ability themselves, they can elect to heal entities within for a combined amount equal to their current Reservoir level.
Partial Success Target entity is damaged at half Potency; damage converted into Reservoir at a 1:1 ratio.
Full Success Target entity is damaged at full Potency; damage converted into Reservoir at a 1:1 ratio.
Plus Target entity is afflicted with a stack of Status Effect: Impaired.
Lifeburn
Unique Action | Harmonics Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation / Attack | - Defense, Harmonics
Prerequisite Harvest
Description The caster draws upon their own Vitality to power an attack against an entity that bypasses any remaining Shield they have. Vitality spent this way must be elected to be spent prior to rolling the check. Once a caster uses this ability, they must be restored to full Vitality before they can cast it again.
Partial Success Caster converts Vitality to damage at a 1:1 ratio.
Full Success Caster converts Vitality to damage at a 1:half Potency ratio.
Plus Caster converts Vitality to damage at a 1:Potency ratio.
Vital Surge
Standard Action | Harmonics Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Harmonics
Prerequisite Regenerate
Description Heal all targets the caster chooses within range. Healing beyond a target's maximum Vitality may be applied to their Shield.
Partial Success Target entities are healed at half Potency.
Full Success Target entities are healed at full Potency.
Plus Remove a status effect from each target entity.

The College of Occlusion

First Spell Tier

Ward
Standard Action | Occlusion Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Occlusion
Prerequisite none
Description The caster bolsters their Shield's architecture, providing a bonus to defense against all types of effects. Effect ends and will need to be reapplied once Shield has dropped.
Partial Success Target entity's Defense receives a static bonus equal to half Potency.
Full Success Target entity's Defense receives a static bonus equal to full Potency.
Plus While the effect lasts, any hostile action taken against the target entity triggers a Retaliatory Strike.
Fortify
Standard Action | Occlusion Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Occlusion
Prerequisite none
Description Alter your Shield's architecture, rendering it nearly impervious to attacks. Damage dealt to the target while the shield is active is reduced by half (minimum 1); this barrier lasts until the target's Shield is depleted, or until they take any hostile or magical action.
Partial Success Target entity receives a Defense bonus equal to caster's Potency.
Full Success All incoming hostile effects against target entity receive a -2 d20 Polarity penalty.
Plus Target's Defense bonus is also increased by an amount equal to their maximum Shield value.

Second Spell Tier

Barrier
Unique Action | Occlusion Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Occlusion
Prerequisite Ward
Description A directional shield that can act as a physical obstruction which inherits the Defense rating of caster which can either be cast and set or actively maintained via a Channel. While Channeled, the Barrier benefits from one Recharge per round for free, and the caster may elect to allow entities to pass through its event horizon without issue.
Partial Success The Barrier instantiates at full Potency, but must be Channeled until at least the end of the Round to persist.
Full Success The Barrier instantiates at double Potency, and is stable without needing to be Channeled.
Plus The Barrier instantiates at triple Potency and benefits from a free Recharge action at the start of every Round even if it is not being maintained via Channel.
Stasis
Channeled Action | Occlusion Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation / Attack, Occlusion | - Defense, Occlusion
Prerequisite none
Description Puts an entity in a magical stasis, akin to being asleep. Hostile actions against an entity held in stasis trigger channel checks with a -5 d20 Polarity penalty.
Partial Success Target entity is Suspended, but retains awareness of their environment and may use actions to Counter the Channel.
Full Success Target entity retains awareness of their environment while Suspended but cannot Counter it themselves.
Plus Target entity is subjected to a free Recharge with the caster's stats immediately and at the start of every Round. If the target elects to not apply their Defense modifier the spell's check, this Recharge is beneficial; if the target's Defense modifier was applied, this Recharge siphons the target's remaining Shield instead, but has no effect on their Vitality.
Bonded Ward
Channeled Action | Occlusion Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Occlusion
Prerequisite Ward
Description Split damage taken with another entity.
Partial Success The caster may absorb up to half their Potency in damage from every hostile action against target entity.
Full Success The caster may absorb up to their full Potency in damage from every hostile action against target entity.
Plus The caster may absorb as much damage as they choose from each hostile action against target entity, and mitigate half the damage they absorb.

Third Spell Tier

Nullification Field
Channeled Action | Occlusion Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Occlusion
Prerequisite Phalanx
Description Create a spherical barrier with a radius of up to 4 units which partitions the area within off from the rest of the Flow: the formation of spell architecture is prevented inside, and external architectures unravel upon contact with its event horizon. The field instantiates regardless, but a check must be rolled for every entity within or that enters if they are maintaining any magical effects in order to determine if they are stripped of their enchantments. Falter/Failure degrees on these checks trigger an immediate Channel check against the caster, but targets within are Suppressed regardless of Degree so long as the field holds.
Partial Success Afflict target entity with Status Effect: Suppressed, triggering any applicable Channel checks; any maintained Channeled abilities are suspended.
Full Success The caster may choose to terminate any Channeled ability on Suppressed targets regardless of any Channel check.
Plus The caster may detonate any magical effects Suppressed or terminated by this effect for free, dealing damage at half Potency to entities within 1 unit.
Phalanx
Channeled Action | Occlusion Spell
Modifiers + Adaptation, Occlusion
Prerequisite Barrier
Description A fully enclosed Barrier centered around the caster with a radius of up to their Ability Range that replenishes and empowers those the caster marks as allies, and hinders those it views as hostile for as long as it lasts.
Partial Success The phalanx instantiates at double Potency. Allies inside the Phalanx benefit from a free Recharge immediately upon instantiation/entry, and at the start of every Round they remain inside.
Full Success The phalanx instantiates at 4x Potency. Hostile entities inside are unable to execute Recharge actions.
Plus The phalanx instantiates at 6x Potency. All effects targeting the phalanx and/or allied entities within receive a Defense bonus at full Potency.

Projection School Architectures

The College of Conduction

First Spell Tier

Buoy
Channeled Action | Conduction Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Conduction
Prerequisite none
Description The caster warps the Flow around them, controlling their rate of descent.
Partial Success The caster comfortably descends at a rate of 1 unit per Round while falling, though they may spend Movement to descend faster; they will not take impact damage from falling.
Full Success Movement undertaken by the caster while falling may be horizontal.
Plus While falling, the caster may choose to hover in place for free; if they elect to move at any point during a Round before hovering, they will still fall 1 unit before halting their downward momentum to hover.
Expulsion
Standard Action | Conduction Spell
Modifiers + Attack / Projection, Conduction | - Defense, Conduction
Prerequisite none
Description A concentrated blast of force that can send entities flying.
Partial Success Damage target entity at partial Potency.
Full Success Damage target entity at full Potency.
Plus Move target entity a number of units equal to damage dealt in a direction elected by the caster.
Jolt
Standard Action | Conduction Spell
Modifiers + Attack / Projection, Conduction | - Defense, Conduction
Prerequisite none
Description A discharge of energy that can Addle targets.
Partial Success Damage target entity at half Potency.
Full Success Damage target entity at full Potency.
Plus Afflict target entity with Status Effect: Addled.

Second Spell Tier

Arc Wave
Standard Action | Conduction Spell
Modifiers + Attack / Projection, Conduction | - Defense, Conduction
Prerequisite Expulsion
Description A wave of force 3 units wide and a length equal to the caster’s Potency which moves affected entities a number of units equal to the damage they receive.
Partial Success Damage target entity at half Potency.
Full Success Damage target entity at full Potency.
Plus The spell gains a +2 Potency bonus, and target entities are moved twice as far.
Flight
Channeled Action | Conduction Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Conduction
Prerequisite Buoy
Description Gain the ability to Fly, using your astral limbs to hold you aloft.
Partial Success The caster gains the ability to fly.
Full Success The caster's movement speed is doubled while flying.
Plus The caster may execute one free Movement action per Round.
Flux Beam
Standard Action | Conduction Spell
Modifiers + Attack / Projection, Conduction | - Defense, Conduction
Prerequisite Jolt
Description A versatile beam of shifting arcane energy, capable of adapting to the caster's strengths and whims.
Partial Success Damage target entity at half Potency.
Full Success Damage target entity at full Potency.
Plus The spell gains a +2 Potency bonus and may inflict any Status Effect the caster has access to from their abilities.

Third Spell Tier

Arc Vortex
Channeled Action | Conduction Spell
Modifiers + Attack / Projection, Conduction | - Defense, Conduction
Prerequisite Fluxion
Description The caster alters the flow of azai in the area to create a localized phenomenon with a radius between 1 unit and their max Potency value. One check is rolled at the top of each Round, which is applied individually to each target that begins the Round inside the effect or enters it at any point. Targets receive stacks of Entangled equal to the damage they are dealt by this ability.
Partial Success Damage target entity and inflict stacks of Status Effect: Entangled at half Potency.
Full Success Damage target entity and inflict stacks of Status Effect: Entangled at full Potency.
Plus The spell receives a +4 Potency bonus.
Chain Reaction
Standard Action | Conduction Spell
Modifiers + Attack / Projection, Conduction | - Defense, Conduction
Prerequisite Arc Wave
Description A projected beam that arcs to additional targets within 2 units. The spell's attack must be re-rolled for each jump, and is capable of bouncing back and forth between two targets; it continues bouncing until a check receives a Failure degree or there are no targets to jump to.
Partial Success Damage target entity at half Potency.
Full Success Damage target entity at full Potency.
Plus The spell gains a +2 Potency bonus for this hit and all future jumps.
Fluxion
Standard Action | Conduction Spell
Modifiers + Attack / Projection, Conduction | - Defense, Conduction
Prerequisite Flux Beam
Description Cause an impact of fluctuating energies in a localized area with a radius equal to the Potency of the caster.
Partial Success Damage target entity at half Potency.
Full Success Damage target entity at full Potency.
Plus The spell inflicts one of any Status Effect the caster has access to from their abilities.

The College of Enchantment

First Spell Tier

Enhance
Channeled Action | Enchantment Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Enchantment
Prerequisite none
Description Alter the azonian structure of an object or entity, enhancing one of its secondary statistical bonuses.
Partial Success Apply a bonus to a Secondary Stat of choice at half Potency.
Full Success Apply a bonus to a Secondary Stat of choice at full Potency.
Plus Apply bonus to the relevant Primary Stat instead of one of its Secondaries.
Glyph
Unique Action | Enchantment Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Enchantment
Prerequisite none
Description Inscribe a glyph to create a magical effect, capable of executing one of the caster's abilities when certain conditions are met. If cast in conjunction with another spell, the spell's effect can be stored within the glyph. There are two checks associated with this ability: one is immediate, to determine how conspicuous and specific its triggers may be, and the second occurs when the glyph is triggered to determine the efficacy of its stored effect(s).
Partial Success The glyph is faintly visible and may store a single conditional trigger.
Full Success The glyph is relatively invisible but detectable if specifically searched for, and may store up to three conditional triggers.
Plus The glyph is virtually undetectable, and any conditional triggers beyond the first may be sacrificed to allow the glyph to trigger its effect an additional time.

Second Spell Tier

Accelerate
Channeled Action | Enchantment Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Enhancement
Prerequisite Enhance
Description Speed up an entity’s movements.
Partial Success Double the target entity's Movement speed.
Full Success Target entity may expend their entire Action Economy as Reactions.
Plus Increase target entity's Action Economy by 1.
Construct
Unique Action | Enchantment Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Enchantment
Prerequisite Glyph
Description Create a small Wisp construct out of pure azonian architecture, able to execute simple commands as part of the caster’s Faction Turn. Caster may only have a single Wisp active at any moment, and the Wisp must remain within the caster's active ability range. If the caster moves in such a way that the Wisp would no longer be within their ability range, the construct is pulled along at the edge of their range as though by an invisible tether.
Partial Success The wisp begins to instantiate, but does not fully coalesce to act until next Round.
Full Success The wisp instantiates and may act immediately.
Plus The wisp's Action Economy increases by 1 for this Round.
Disrupt
Standard Action | Enchantment Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Enchantment | - Defense, Enchantment (conditional)
Prerequisite Construct
Description Unravel spell architecture, either Suppressing or terminating its effect.
Partial Success Interrupt any spells which have not yet taken full effect (e.g. Construct on a Partial Success), OR Suppress an unmaintained active spell effect (e.g. a Barrier that is not being Channeled) for the rest of the Round.
Full Success End or Suppress an effect that is not being actively maintained, OR trigger a Channel check against an entity that is maintaining an effect; if they succeed on their check, the effect is Suppressed until the end of the Round.
Plus The caster may either Suppress or terminate any Channeled effects without triggering a Channel check.
Invest
Standard Action | Enchantment Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Enchantment
Prerequisite Construct
Description Places a permanent conditional enchantment on an item, which produces an effect once certain conditions are met. Unlike Glyph, this enchantment is not expended upon use, making it indispensable in the manufacture of magical items. Any item can only bear a single investiture at a time, but successive applications can overwrite prior enchantments. Mechanically, this applies cause-effect pairs to an object, meaning it will produce an effect once certain conditions are met, for example: when a button is pushed (cause), an alarm will sound (effect); when a password is spoken (cause), a door will open (effect); when a trigger is pulled (cause), then a spell will be cast (effect). Enchantments applied in this way are limited to the capabilities of the caster.
Partial Success The caster applies 1 cause-effect pair to the target object.
Full Success The caster applies 2 cause-effect pairs to the target object.
Plus The caster can mix and match between 6 total causes and effects, or 3 pairs.
Lethargy
Channeled Action | Enchantment Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Enchantment | - Defense, Enchantment
Prerequisite Enhance
Description Slow down the movements of an entity.
Partial Success Halve the target entity's maximum Movement speed.
Full Success Target entity may execute one less Reaction outside their Faction Turn per Round.
Plus Target entity loses one action from their Action Economy per Round.

Third Spell Tier

Arcanomaton
Unique Action | Enchantment Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Enchantment
Prerequisite Construct
Description Create Construct architecture within an inanimate object, imbuing it with the ability to fulfill simple, specific tasks. An arcanomaton summoned by a caster counts against their active Construct limit of 1. If the Caster already has an active Wisp Construct within physical range of the target inanimate object when they cast this spell, they may implant the Wisp into the object and apply a bonus equal to their Potency to the spell's check. Unlike a Wisp Construct, Arcanomatons can continue to function regardless of their distance from the Caster's ability range.
Partial Success The construct instantiates, but has 0 Shield and cannot act until the following Round.
Full Success The construct instantiates with 10 Shield and may act immediately.
Plus The construct's Shield is set to its maximum value.
Dispel
Standard Action | Enchantment Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Enchantment | - Defense, Enchantment
Prerequisite Disrupt
Description Disrupt or unravel specific types of azonian structures within an area with a radius equal to the caster's Potency.
Partial Success Suppress any unmaintained permanent enchantment, terminate any non-permanent enchantments. Dreamers maintaining a Channel make a Channel check; even if they maintain their Channel, its effects are Suppressed until the end of the Round.
Full Success Channel checks triggered by this spell are made with a d20 Polarity penalty equal to half Potency.
Plus Channel check penalty increases to full Potency.
Imbue
Standard Action | Enchantment Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Enchantment
Prerequisite Arcanomaton
Description Imbue a Construct or Invested object with sentience. Sentient constructs maintain an initially friendly demeanor towards the caster, which is maintained unless the caster is hostile or otherwise offensive towards them.
Partial Success The spell takes effect, but Construct will require a full Long Rest before it is able to awaken/act. Any existing memory banks are lost.
Full Success The construct awakens and may act immediately. Any existing memory banks are lost.
Plus The construct awakens with all memories stored in its databanks from before its awakening intact.

The College of Perception

First Spell Tier

Blur
Channeled Action | Perception Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Perception
Prerequisite Illusory Guise
Description Blurs the entity's image. This ability takes effect without a check. When the entity makes a Channel check while maintaining this effect, the result of their Channel check influences the success of the triggering action taken against them.
Partial Success The effect remains active, and no Polarity penalty is applied to future Channel checks.
Full Success The action which triggered the Channel check has its Degree of Success reduced by 1.
Plus The triggering action's Degree of Success is reduced by 2.
Illusory Guise
Standard Action | Perception Spell
Modifiers + Projection, Perception
Prerequisite none
Description The caster affects how others perceive them without physically altering anything.
Partial Success The caster alters a single sensory perception (e.g. how a character looks, or how they smell, but not both).
Full Success The caster alters up to three sensory perceptions.
Plus The caster may alter everything about how they are perceived with all senses.
Sensory Deprivation
Standard Action | Perception Spell
Modifiers + Attack / Projection, Perception | - Defense, Perception
Prerequisite none
Description Deprive an entity of its ability to perceive through their targeted sense(s) for the duration of the spell.
Partial Success Afflict target entity with a single stack of Status Effect: Impaired.
Full Success Afflict target entity with three stacks of Status Effect: Impaired.
Plus Afflict target entity with maximum stacks of Status Effect: Impaired.

Second Spell Tier

Spotlight
Channeled Action | Perception Spell
