Style

A character's style develops around their preferences for wielding their Azaian Focus. Every dreamer wields a focus (imagine a wizard without a wand!), which they acquire at a relatively young age. Foci are usually in the shape/size of a small gem when they receive them during adolescence, though they will often grow and change shape to reflect the preferences of their wielder; some foci are even embedded in larger items, for both functional and decorative purposes. A character's choice of style will govern the ways in which they employ magic, the Potency of their spells, the range at which they are effective, and the status effects they can inflict with attacks.

Potency

A character's base Potency is derived from their choice of Style. Every other ability they use or spell they cast is subject to, and scales with, their Potency. A character's base Potency, and increases to it, will always be an even value in order to allow damage calculations to be easily halved when a character meets with a Partial Success on an attack.

Dual Strikes

Passive Bonus

Speed is the hallmark of this style. Every check involving the caster's Potency is made twice. Status effects applied on a Plus can only be applied by a single one of these checks; if both result in a Plus, the check which is unable to apply a status effect receives a +2 bonus to its Potency.

Projected Focus

Passive Bonus

Manipulation of one's Focus is central to this style. Unlike other styles, the dreamer does not need to be physically wielding their focus in order to channel their magic through it: using their telekinetics, the dreamer may wield their focus anywhere within their ability range. When separated, the wielder may choose either themselves or their focus as the point of origin for any of their abilities. Actions executed by the wielder’s focus have an ability range equivalent to the wielder and behave as though they were physically in that location, effectively increasing the wielder’s spell range.

While separate from its dreamer, the focus cannot be damaged or destroyed. However, if it is targeted with a successful attack that might achieve such ends, it immediately teleports back to its dreamer for free.

Mounting Strikes

Passive Bonus

The first hostile action taken each Round receives a +1 d20 Polarity bonus. This bonus stacks, increasing by 1 for every additional hostile action taken beyond the first each Round; if the dreamer's Polarity is maxed, additional stacks instead confer a +2 Potency bonus. These stacks reset at the beginning of every Round.

Spellmark Style

A long range style focused around careful observation and calculated applications of force.

Potency 4
Range Bonus 5 units

Standard Action | Skill
Modifiers + Attack, Conduction | - Defense
Description The dreamer accepts a -1 Polarity penalty to both dice pools in order to split the force of their attack into multiple vectors, striking as many targets within range as they choose.
Partial Success Target entity suffers damage equal to half Potency.

Measured Shot
Unique Action | Skill
Description This ability does not cost an Action to use, and is instead used in combination with the action it is modifying. The dreamer takes a moment to line up their next attack, which is made with a base Potency of 6, and a +1 Polarity bonus to both dice pools. If a dice pool's Polarity is capped before this bonus is applied, the base Potency increases by an additional 2. This ability can only be used once per Round, and only if the dreamer has not taken any movement that Round. Once used, they cannot take any Movement of their own accord until the following Round.
