Arcane Artistry

Pioneered by a partnership between the dragons Azyra and Agirot with the Eight Immortals of Keshal, arcanotech relies upon embedding layers of permanent spell architecture within an object. This technology is responsible for the creation of almost all magical items, which have become ubiquitous and multi-faceted as demanded by the experiences of an interstellar civilization.

Foci

Of all the wonders invented via arcanotech, none is so crucial or ubiquitous to every dreamer's life as their focus. They serve to assist dreamers in the stabilization of azonian architecture, making their spells much more effective and reliable. The aurion cores used in their construction allows a focus' power to grow in tandem with its wielder, and most regard their focus as an extension of themself. Truly powerful dreamers may even find that their focus has awakened to a sentience of its own.

Unitools

Within Thymora, unitools are nearly as widespread as foci. Typically worn or implanted around the wrist, they expand and enhance a dreamer's natural psionics, granting them the ability to communicate long-distance via telepathic and/or holographic channels, regulate their internal azai to produce a naturally sustained protective shield, and even store information and single-use spell architectures. Beyond this, they also allow the bearer to interact with Thymoran technology like access terminals, teleportation systems, vehicles, etc..

Talismans

A relatively generic catch-all, the term talisman can refer to any form of enchanted item that requires its wielder to power it. Foci and unitools are the most obvious examples of talisman, but others, such as an artisan's Shaper's Tools, or an Invisibility Cloak, are no less iconic.

Arcanetics

As technology surrounding talismans grew and became more adaptable, they eventually spawned the field of arcanetics: the implantation of enchanted materials into the body. Common arcanetics function similar to worn or wielded talismans, and they often appear as arcane tattoos or circuity on the skin or as prosthetic limbs entirely.

Vehicles

Reminiscent of the cloudy loft pads of psionic energy popularized by Arcanists, flying vehicles are often used by dreamers for travel. Their shapes and sizes range from a personal surfboard to flying villas, though they do tend towards the smaller variants.

Assimilation Engines

Harnessing the power of transmutation magic, assimilation engines are capable of converting raw materials into almost any configuration. They function as industrial 3D printers, and are the backbone of Thymoran manufacturing.

Arcanomatons

Though not widespread, constructs are made and employed for all sorts of tasks. Now that a faction of them have gained sentience and freedom, however, it is nearly impossible to predict how they will evolve in the coming years.
