Dreamscape Setting Codex

This is the landing for the Dreamscape Setting. Each section of the codex is listed below with a link and small descriptor.

The Flow

The Dreamscape arises from the fundamental force: Azonai. While this is held as general knowledge, the Azaian Flow is generally used as a symbol for it, due to its ubiquitous nature.

The Dreamscape

The Dreamscape is comprised of three interconnected and interdependent dimensions: the Dream, the Oracle, and the Reliquary. It is understood that the three dimensions are, in a very real sense, manifested by the circulation of the deepest Flow currents from within the Interstice, and maintain their equilibrium so long as the flow of azai remains balanced between them.

Entities

Living beings with self awareness are referred to as entities within the Dreamscape. While there is generally a large variety in the manifestations of the many different forms of life, all entities share two traits that are specific to their classification: astral bodies, and attunements.

Arcanotech

Pioneered by a partnership between the dragons Azyra and Agirot with the Eight Immortals of Keshal, arcanotech relies upon embedding layers of permanent spell architecture within an object. This technology is responsible for the creation of almost all magical items, which have become ubiquitous and multi-faceted as demanded by the experiences of an interstellar civilization.

Thymora

Thymora is a federation of courts across many realms and star systems of the Dreamscape, promoting secure and equitable relations among its members. Thanks to a permissive democratic structure, robust trade and transit networks, and a stable central authority, it has met with remarkable success. As the sole civilization capable of surviving and defeating the Grey Tide, Thymorans proved their martial acumen many times over. Even so, the Archonate has refrained from creating long-term enemies via aggression, affording them a respectable reputation even among those courts who retain their sovereignty. From planetary realms, to space stations maintaining stable paths through wormholes, realms manufactured wholecloth by unknown progenitors, or orbital platforms surveying mysterious interstellar phenomena, there are few places among the known Dream that have not been charted or protected by Thymora.

History

The historical eras of the Dreamscape fall in one of two categories: Uncharted Eras(UE), and Charted Eras(CE). The Charted Eras begin with Fengara's awakening and continue from there, as no post-Celebrant society has yet been found to originate earlier, and generally account for all of recorded history. The Uncharted Eras are from a previous cycle of civilization which, though we have heard first-hand accounts of those times, are so far removed from now that almost no physical evidence remains to support the stories we have heard.

The Starsong Calendar

The realms of the Dream are all witness to the same fluctuations within the Azaian Flow, which cycles at a predictable rate throughout the Dream's entirety. The reliability of this phenomenon, as well as the wonder it inspires, has served as the foundation for the calendar of every known civilization encounted by Thymora. Though days progress differently on different realms, the standard of the Thymoran calendar has been easily adopted.

Dramatis Personae

The Dream has been shaped by the entities within it, many of whom have left enduring marks. This list is by no means exhaustive, but does its best to account for as many prominent figures in the setting as possible.
