Dimensions

The Dreamscape is comprised of three interconnected and interdependent dimensions: the Dream, the Oracle, and the Reliquary. It is understood that the three dimensions are, in a very real sense, manifested by the circulation of the deepest Flow currents from within the Interstice, and maintain their equilibrium so long as the flow of azai remains balanced between them.
